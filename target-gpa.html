How the target GPA calculation works

Your cumulative GPA is a weighted average. To find what you need going forward, we calculate the total quality points your goal requires, subtract your current points, and divide by remaining credits.

The formula is: required GPA = (goal × total credits − current GPA × completed credits) ÷ remaining credits.
