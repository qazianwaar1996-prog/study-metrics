How to calculate percentages

A percentage is a fraction out of 100. To find the percentage of a score, divide the obtained marks by the total possible marks and multiply by 100.

How do I convert marks to a percentage?

Divide your marks by the total possible, then multiply by 100. The "Marks → %" tab does it instantly.
