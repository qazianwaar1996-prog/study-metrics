How to calculate a percentage

A percentage is a fraction out of 100. Divide what you got by the maximum possible, then multiply by 100. So 47 out of 60 is (47 ÷ 60) × 100 = 78.33%.

How do I convert marks to a percentage?

Divide your marks by the total possible, then multiply by 100. The "Marks → %" tab does it instantly.
