How a weighted grade is calculated

The formula is: grade = Σ(score × weight) ÷ Σ(weight). Because we divide by the total weight entered, it works even before all your grades are in.

Do my weights need to add up to 100%?

Not necessarily. If they total less, you get your grade for the portion completed so far.

Is anything saved or uploaded?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser.
