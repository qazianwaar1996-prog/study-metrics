How a weighted grade is calculated

Your final course grade is a weighted blend of your assignments. The formula is: grade = Σ(score × weight) ÷ Σ(weight).

Do my weights need to add up to 100%?

Not necessarily. If they total less, you get your grade for the portion completed so far, which is normal mid-semester.
