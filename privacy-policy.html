Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Study Metrics ("we", "us", "our") is built on a simple idea: the tools should work without us knowing anything about you. This policy explains what little data is involved when you use our website, how cookies and advertising work, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We designed Study Metrics to collect as little as possible. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, or any personal details to use the calculators and tools on this site.

The numbers you enter into our tools, such as grades, credits, budgets or the text in the word counter, are processed entirely within your browser. They are never transmitted to our servers. We simply never receive them.

The limited information that may be collected automatically, through third-party services like advertising and analytics, is described in the sections below.

2. Data stored on your device

To make the tools more convenient, some of them save your inputs in your browser's local storage. For example, your GPA courses or an in-progress essay in the word counter may be remembered so a page refresh doesn't wipe your work.

This data stays on your own device and is not accessible to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data, or by using the "Clear" buttons built into the tools.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. Study Metrics itself does not use cookies to track you. However, third-party services we rely on, principally advertising, may set cookies. These are used to serve and measure ads, and to limit how often you see the same ad.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect how ads are shown but will not stop the tools from working.

4. Advertising and Google AdSense

Study Metrics is free to use and is supported by advertising. We use Google AdSense to display ads on this site. Google is a third-party vendor and uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info/choices.

For more on how Google uses data when you use our site, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

5. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand which tools are popular and how the site performs, so we can improve it. Any such data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. We do not sell or share this information.

6. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (Europe) or CCPA (California), including the right to access, correct or delete personal data held about you. Because we do not collect personal data through the tools themselves, most requests relate to cookies set by third parties, which you control through the opt-out links above and your browser settings.

If you have a specific request, contact us using the details below and we will respond in line with applicable law.

7. Children's privacy

Study Metrics is intended for a general student audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will address it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be highlighted on the site where appropriate.

9. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your privacy? Reach us through our contact page or email hello@studymetrics.app.
