What is CGPA?

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is the weighted average of your grades across every semester of your program.

Why credits matter

A common mistake is averaging semester GPAs directly. Because credit loads vary, a heavy 18-credit term weighs more than a light 9-credit one.
