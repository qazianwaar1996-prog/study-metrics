What is CGPA and how is it calculated?

CGPA is the weighted average of your grades across every semester, not just one term. It reflects your overall academic standing for the whole degree.

  • Multiply each semester's GPA by its credits to get its quality points.
  • Add up the quality points from every semester.
  • Divide by the total credits across all semesters.
