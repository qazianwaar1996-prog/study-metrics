About Study Metrics

Built for students, by people who remember the grind

Study Metrics started with a simple frustration: the tools students actually need are scattered across a dozen cluttered websites, buried under pop-ups, forced logins and paywalls. We wanted one calm, fast home for all of them.

Every tool here is free to use, works instantly in your browser, and never asks you to create an account. Your inputs, whether that's your GPA, your grades or your assignment deadlines, stay on your own device. We don't need them, so we don't collect them.

How we keep it free

Study Metrics is supported by unobtrusive advertising. Ads help cover hosting and let us keep every tool completely free, with no subscriptions and no locked features.
