How to calculate your GPA

Your Grade Point Average (GPA) is a weighted average of your grades. A course worth 4 credits affects your GPA twice as much as a 2-credit course.

Is this GPA calculator free?

Yes, completely. No account or signup required.

What is the difference between GPA and CGPA?

GPA is for one term/semester. CGPA is cumulative for your whole degree.
