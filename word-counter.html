Why word count matters for students

Most essays come with a strict word limit. This counter provides a real-time breakdown of your writing, helping you ensure you meet requirements without going over. Reading time is estimated at an average speed of 200 words per minute.

Does it count words as I type?

Yes, all statistics update instantly with every keystroke. There is no limit to the length of the text you can analyze.

Is my text secure?

Your draft is saved locally in your browser's memory (local storage). Nothing is ever uploaded to a server or seen by anyone else.
