Why word count matters for students

Most essays come with a word limit. This counter gives you a live count plus sentences, paragraphs and average sentence length to help tighten your writing. Reading time is estimated at ~200 words per minute.

Does it count words as I type?

Yes, everything updates instantly. No button, no length limit.

Is my text saved or uploaded?

Your draft is saved only in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.
