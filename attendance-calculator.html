How attendance percentage works

Your attendance percentage is simply the classes you attended divided by the total classes held, times 100. Most colleges set a minimum, commonly 75%, that you must maintain to sit exams.

The bunk math

To find safe skips, we solve for the largest number of future absences that keeps your ratio above the requirement. To recover, we calculate the streak of upcoming classes needed to hit your goal.

What's the usual minimum attendance?
